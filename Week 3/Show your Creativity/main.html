About Us

The travel, tourism and leisure industry is undergoing dramatic change: consumers are now calling the shots. It’s down to the invention of new technologies and the sharing economy, which have given customers flexibility, control and new expectations. There’s also been a shift in the pattern of demand, as millennials become a force to be reckoned with and the emerging economies open up new markets.
